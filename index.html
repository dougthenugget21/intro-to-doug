Intro to Doug

Hello there, I'm Doug!
I'm a curious person with a creative mind, and I spend my time playing video games, watching movies, and always looking for ways to add to my never ending collection of geeky memorabilia. In my spare time I also take care of my pets. I like to stay up to date with the latest science news, and I'm fascinated by new technologies, animal discoveries, space news, and of course, everything and anything about dinosaurs.

Animals, Cosplay & Too Many Collectibles

When I was younger, I told myself I wanted to be a mad scientist and create amazing things. Little did I know I'd actually get the chance to become one.

As I grew older, I realised animals were a real passion. I'd always catch myself watching ants trailing by, bees drinking nectar, or birds flying around the city. That curiosity led me to study Animal Studies, turning my passion and knowledge into something bigger. Now I get to apply what I learned every day with my own pets: Squirtle, my rescued map turtle, and Jupiter, my leopard gecko.

When I'm not caring for my pets, I'm usually hunting for a new interest or hobby, planning a cosplay, or searching for new figures, statues or helmets to add to my growing geeky collection.
